# Fisher Information

**Domain:** Information Theory

**Equation:** `I(θ)ᵢⱼ = E[(∂log p/∂θᵢ)(∂log p/∂θⱼ)]  ≥  1/Var(θ̂)  (Cramér-Rao)`

**Update Form:** curvature_of_likelihood

**Optimization:** minimize_estimation_variance

**Fixed Points:** cramer_rao_bound
