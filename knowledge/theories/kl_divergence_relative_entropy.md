# KL Divergence / Relative Entropy

**Domain:** Information Theory

**Equation:** `D_KL(P‖Q) = Σ p(x) log[p(x)/q(x)] ≥ 0`

**Update Form:** measure_of_distribution_difference

**Optimization:** minimize_kl_to_target

**Fixed Points:** P = Q

## Patterns

- asymmetric_distance
- information_gain
- prediction_error
- surprise
- variational_inference

## Operators

- expectation
- logarithm
- non_negative
- ratio
