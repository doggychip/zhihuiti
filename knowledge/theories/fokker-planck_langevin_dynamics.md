# Fokker-Planck / Langevin Dynamics

**Domain:** Statistical Physics

**Equation:** `∂p/∂t = −∂/∂x[μ(x)p] + (1/2)∂²/∂x²[σ²(x)p];  dx = μdt + σdW`

**Update Form:** drift_diffusion

**Fixed Points:** stationary_distribution
