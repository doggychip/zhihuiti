# General Equilibrium (Arrow-Debreu)

**Domain:** Economics

**Equation:** `Σᵢ xᵢ(p) = Σᵢ ωᵢ;  xᵢ(p) = argmax uᵢ(x) s.t. p·x ≤ p·ωᵢ;  ∃p*: z(p*) = 0`

**Update Form:** tatonnement_price_adjustment

**Optimization:** maximize_utility_under_budget

**Fixed Points:** walrasian_equilibrium_prices
